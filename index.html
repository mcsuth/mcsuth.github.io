ALERTminow

"Alertminow" is a website that allows you to keep track of items that you are searching for on CraigsList. Simply add an item to track and you will receive daily email reminders with CraigsList links in the body of the email. Once you find/obtain your item, all you need to do it remove the item from your tracking list and the email reminders will stop.
